## Action Flow

### High Priority Actions (Every Game)
```
1. setRoom() - When joining game
2. setDeck() - When game starts
3. setFirstPlayer() - Determine who goes first
4. startTurn() - Begin your turn
5. playCardFromHandToField() - Play cards
6. endTurn() - End your turn
7. receiveTurnStart() - Receive turn from opponent
8. receiveTurnEnd() - Opponent starts their turn
```

### Medium Priority Actions (Common)
```
9. drawFromDeck() - Draw cards
10. addTempPlayPoints() - Card effects
11. addPermanentPlayPoints() - Card effects
12. setEnemyPlayPoints() - Sync opponent state
```

### Low Priority Actions (Special Cases)
```
13. manualSetPlayPoints() - Emergency adjustments
14. Various field manipulation actions
15. Cemetery/banish actions
16. Evolution actions
```
